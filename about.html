About

My name is Joshua. I'm a Christian book artist based out of the High Country of North Carolina.

My work uses (non-AI based) generative processes to visualize and express in new ways the depth, power, and beauty of the texts that characterize the great Christian tradition. I draw on a combination of theology, church history, and contemporary scholarship to inform my projects.

Please reach out with any inquires. God bless.
